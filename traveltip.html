Travel Tips

Packing Essentials

Carry light cotton clothes, sunscreen, power bank, first aid kit and comfortable shoes.

Budget Travel

Use trains, local transport and book tickets early to save money during your trip.

Safety Tips

Keep valuables secure, follow local customs and always carry bottled water.

Cultural Etiquette

Respect traditions, remove shoes at temples and dress modestly at sacred places.

Transportation

Travel using metro, buses and trains. Auto-rickshaws are useful for short rides.

Best Seasons

October to March is ideal for exploring most destinations across India.

Solo Travel

Kerala, Himachal and Pondicherry are popular and safe destinations for solo trips.

Photography Tips

Capture sunrise and sunset views, local culture and colorful street life.
